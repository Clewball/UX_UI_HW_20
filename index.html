Greenhouse

Plant Care. Simplified.

Introduction

When someone is new to plant ownership, knowing when and how much water can be frustrating and overwhelming. We intend to take all of the complicated parts of plant ownership and make them as easy as taking a photo and setting reminders.

Survey & Interview Data

Our approach to research inluded three zoom interviews with plant owners as well as a survey of over 100 people about their experinces with plants. Some of the information we learned includes the following points.

86%

Of the people we surveyed own anywhere from 1 to 10 plants

65%

Of the people surveyed own plants to bring happiness and aesthetics into their home

95%

Surveyed admitted they don’t know how to care for their plants correctly and have killed at least one plant

Competitive Analysis

We identified three mobile apps that would be in direct competition with Greenhouse through our survey asking which apps plant owners prefer and why. We then conducted a SWOT analysis, created a priority matirx, and identified four opportunities to build on.

Planta Vera Gardenia
Identify Plant Through Photographs
Discovery Page
Free Watering Schedule
Earn Rewards for Keeping Plants Alive

Problem Statement

We observed that most plant owners struggle to keep their plants alive, which is causing them to feel inadequate as plant parents. How might we inform plant parents with the information they need to better care for their plants?

Testing & Iterations

After user testing we decided to change the user flow, eliminating a lengthy onboarding process to go right into the task of adding a plant.

Removed ‘sun’ progress symbol and replaced it with more recognized progression indicator

We also added the ability to swipe out of onboarding & discover pages

Based on feedback from testing we simplified our navigation bar
